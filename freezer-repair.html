Typical Sub-Zero Freezer Issues

  • Warm drawer or high-temp alarm after a door was left open
  • Evaporator frosting or moisture on shelves due to failed heaters
  • Condenser fan noise or vibration in older BI series cabinets
  • Ice maker leaking into the freezer bin
  • Vacuum condenser light that will not clear
  • Control board error codes like EC40, EC24, or EC50

We log pressure readings and fault codes for each visit so returning clients get faster approvals and cleaner service records.
